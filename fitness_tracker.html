💪 Monday — Push Day | Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
Goal: Build chest width & shoulder size. Progressive overload — add 1.25–2.5 kg per week when you complete all reps with good form. Warm-up: 10 min mobility + 2 light warm-up sets before first exercise.
⚠️ Until July 2026: Attend F45 class. Focus on Back & Biceps movements. From August onwards, run this full session at your gym.
💪 Wednesday — Pull Day | Back · Biceps · Rear Delts
Goal: Build a strong back to support posture & muscle mass. Pull-ups are your north star — track max reps each week. Warm-up: 10 min mobility + band pull-aparts × 20.
⚠️ Until July 2026: Attend F45 class. Focus on Squat, Deadlift & Lunge patterns. From August onwards, run this full session at your gym.
💪 Friday — Leg Day | Quads · Hamstrings · Glutes · Calves
Goal: Legs = largest muscle group = most metabolic impact. Squat & RDL are your primary movers. Heavy legs = faster fat loss. Warm-up: 5 min bike/row + 2 goblet squats + hip circles.

🔥 VO2 Max Intervals (After Week 8)
What are VO2 Max Intervals? 4–6 minute efforts at ~95% max HR (171 bpm for you). These are harder than regular intervals. Do 3–5 reps with equal rest. Add 1× per month from Week 9.
